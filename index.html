Introduction

We are students from Reuben College, University of Oxford. We are launching a series of podcast with students engaged in multi-disciplinary research. The interviews will feature students from University of Oxford and around the world. We hope the podcast can inspire and motivate younger generations to pursue their own research interests in multi-disciplinary domains. This project receive funds from Reuben PER Innovation Fund.
